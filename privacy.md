The short version: this site collects very little, and we like it that way. Here is the whole picture, in plain language.

## What we collect, and why

**Nothing we set out to collect when you just read.** This is a static site. We run no advertising, no tracking pixels, and we set no cookies of our own on public pages.

**Hosting and delivery logs.** The site is served by GitHub Pages through Cloudflare. Like almost every website, those providers keep standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested) to run and protect the service. Their handling is governed by [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) and [Cloudflare's privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

**Newsletter signup.** If you subscribe, we store your email address and use it for one thing: sending you the newsletter. We don't sell it, share it, or enrich it. Every issue has an unsubscribe link, and unsubscribing removes you.

**Gated library access.** Parts of the [recipe library](https://library.alliwanlabs.com) require a login. Access is managed through Cloudflare Access, which processes your email address to verify you're on the access list and to log sign-ins. We see who accessed the gated area and when; we don't see more than that.

**Email you send us.** If you write to us, we keep the correspondence for as long as it's useful for answering you and for our records.

## What we don't do

We don't sell personal data. We don't run third-party advertising or analytics trackers. We don't build profiles of readers. If any of this ever changes, this page will say so clearly before it does.

## Your choices

- **Unsubscribe** from the newsletter anytime via the link in any issue.
- **Ask what we hold about you**, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it: email [contact@alliwanlabs.com](mailto:contact@alliwanlabs.com). We'll answer within 30 days.

## Where data lives

GitHub and Cloudflare operate globally, so log data may be processed outside your country. The little data we hold directly (newsletter list, correspondence) is managed from the United Arab Emirates.

## Changes

If we change this policy, we update this page and the date at the top, and flag material changes prominently.
