Al Liwan Labs is a wholly-owned entity of [Al Liwan Group](https://alliwan.com), based in Abu Dhabi. Our job is to develop and test AI capabilities at arm's length from the group's core operating business.

The point of the arm's length is simple: the business adopts only what has been proven, and never has to move faster than it safely should. The Lab can experiment freely precisely because it sits outside that operating business, not inside it.

## What we do

We build and test agentic AI workflows in a ring-fenced setting, compliant by construction. Only what survives that testing is offered to the wider group. The patterns that prove themselves in production become recipes — each one explicit about when it works and how it fails.

## Who we are

Al Liwan Labs is led by [David Orban](https://davidorban.com), Managing Director. The Lab works in the open where it can: our recipes, methods, and the reasoning behind them are documented as we go.

## Where we are

Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates — where AI adoption is not a someday project. The UAE has embraced agentic AI at the level of the state, and the institutions that will matter are the ones building the capability now.
